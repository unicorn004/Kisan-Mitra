Bajra

Discover the wholesome goodness of our organic Bajra grain, also known as Pearl Millet. Sourced from certified organic farms and cultivated with care, our Bajra grain is a nutritious addition to your pantry. Packed with essential nutrients, including iron, magnesium, and fiber, Bajra is a versatile grain that can be used in a variety of culinary creations. From traditional Indian rotis and porridges to modern salads and desserts, Bajra adds a nutty flavor and hearty texture to any dish.
