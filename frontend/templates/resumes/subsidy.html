Central Sector Schemes

1. Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Purpose: To supplement financial needs of land-holding farmers.

Details: PM-KISAN transfers Rs. 6000/year in three installments via DBT to farmers.

Impact: Rs. 2.81 lakh crores transferred to over 11 crore beneficiaries.

Apply Now

2. Pradhan Mantri Kisan MaanDhan Yojana (PM-KMY)

Purpose: Provide security to vulnerable farmer families.

Details: Contributory scheme with monthly subscriptions and pension benefits.

Enrollment: 23.38 lakh farmers have enrolled.

Apply Now

3. Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Purpose: Provide crop insurance cover to farmers.

Details: Offers comprehensive risk cover against natural risks.

Impact: 5549.40 lakh farmer applications insured; Rs. 150589.10 crore paid as claim.

Apply Now

4. Modified Interest Subvention Scheme (MISS)

Purpose: Provide concessional agri-loans to farmers.

Details: Interest rate of 7% per annum; additional subvention for prompt repayment.

Impact: 465.42 lakh new KCC applications sanctioned with credit limit of Rs. 5,69,974 crore.

Apply Now

5. Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

Purpose: Address infrastructure gaps and mobilize investment in agriculture.

Details: Rs. 1 Lakh Crore fund with interest subvention and credit guarantee support.

Impact: Rs. 33.209 Crores sanctioned for 44,912 projects, mobilizing Rs. 56.471 Crores investment.

Apply Now

6. Formation & Promotion of new 10,000 FPOs

Purpose: Promote and support Farmer Producer Organizations.

Details: Financial assistance, equity grant, credit guarantee, and training support.

Impact: Total 7,774 FPOs registered under the scheme.

Apply Now

7. National beekeeping and Honey Mission (NBHM)

Purpose: Promote scientific beekeeping and honey production.

Details: Approval of honeybees as 5th input for agriculture, establishment of testing labs, and online registration portal.

Impact: 23 lakhs bee colonies registered; 160 projects sanctioned under NBHM.

Apply Now
